✦ Play & Compete

Challenges & Games

Keep things fun and fresh across the distance — and in person.

🍣
The Food Ranking Wars
Easy
Each month, both independently rank your top 5 favourite foods eaten that month. Compare lists and argue passionately. The person with the most unique picks wins bragging rights.
🎮
PS5 Tournament
Easy
Pick a game with head-to-head modes. Play a best-of-5 series. Keep a running score across visits. Current champion gets to choose what to eat next date night. No mercy.
🎲
Board Game Championship
Medium
Every visit, play a new board game neither of you has tried. Keep a log. Rate each one. By the end of the year, crown your favourite and buy it for your future home.
🎬
Korean Drama Bracket
Easy
Nominate 8 dramas/movies each. Run a bracket — watch head-to-head and vote on a winner each round. Crown an ultimate champion drama.
💰
Dream Fund Challenge
Medium
Both contribute a small amount monthly to a shared "dream fund" (holiday, ring, experience). Track it together. Saving becomes a love language.
🏆
Who Knows Who Better?
Medium
Quarterly quiz — each writes 10 questions about themselves. Answer on video call. Keep a running score. Loser plans the next virtual date.
💍
The Marriage Planning Sprint
Bold
Once a quarter, dedicate a full "planning date" to the future — venue ideas, timelines, finances, living city. Every conversation makes it more real.

Jeju Island — Hydrangea Photoshoot
Visit in late June to early July when Jeju's blue and purple hydrangeas are at absolute peak bloom. Best spots: Camellia Hill in Seogwipo and Hallim Park — both famous for thousands of purple and blue hydrangea bushes perfect for a couple photoshoot. The flowers bloom more vivid after summer rain 💜.
